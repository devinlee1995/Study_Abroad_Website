My Year Abroad

My study abroad experience at Kobe University in Japan.

Hello! My name is Devin Lee, and for Fall 2016 and Spring 2017, I studied abroad at Kobe University in Japan. This website describes my journey with bi-monthly blog posts and photo collections. I absolutely enjoyed my experience abroad, and I would have not made it there if it was not for the Macaulay Honors College, Queens College, Kobe University, and the Benjamin A. Gilman International Scholarship.
